Instagram Feed

Connect your Instagram to show real posts on the homepage. You'll need a Meta (Facebook) App with Instagram Basic Display access.

How to get your Instagram token
  1. Go to developers.facebook.com and create a Meta App (type: Consumer)
  2. Add the Instagram Basic Display product
  3. Under Basic Display → Add Instagram Tester, add your Instagram account
  4. Accept the tester invite on Instagram (Settings → Apps and Websites)
  5. Generate a token in the Basic Display settings panel
  6. Exchange it for a long-lived token (valid 60 days) — the tool below can refresh it
