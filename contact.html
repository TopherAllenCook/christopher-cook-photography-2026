Ask a Question
How far in advance should I book? +
Most couples book 12–18 months before their wedding date. Popular summer and fall weekends in Park City and destination locations fill quickly — if your date is within the next six months, reach out right away and we'll let you know our availability.
Do you travel for destination weddings? +
Yes — travel is built into our DNA. We've photographed weddings across Italy, France, Greece, Mexico, and throughout the U.S. Travel expenses (flights, lodging, transport) are billed at cost and included in your custom proposal.
What does your editing process look like? +
Every image is individually refined — not batch-processed. We work to a consistent aesthetic: natural skin tones, luminous light, timeless tonality. Digital files are delivered alongside any medium-format film scans through a private online gallery.
How long until we receive our gallery? +
Your full gallery is delivered within six weeks of your wedding date. You'll receive a preview of 10–15 images within 72 hours — something to hold on to while we finalize the full collection.
Do you offer albums? +
Yes. We offer hand-bound heirloom albums crafted by artisans trusted by the world's top portrait photographers. Albums can be added to any collection and are designed collaboratively — you'll approve every spread before it goes to print.
Can we meet before booking? +
Absolutely — we encourage it. We offer a no-obligation video call for couples who want to get a feel for working together before committing. Fill out the inquiry form and mention you'd like to connect, and we'll schedule a call.
